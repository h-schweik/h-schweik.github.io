ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Harry Schweikert!

I'm a soon-to-be graduate of Macquarie University with a Bachelor of Media and Communications. As of now, I'm looking to be as versatile as I can within the media industry.

Stylistically speaking, the early 2000s has been a key-note influence of mine, a time when media was beginning to reach new heights, experimentation was around every corner, and creatives were free to be bold and 'out-there' in what they created.

To bring the bold and 'out-there' into the new era is what I strive to do.
